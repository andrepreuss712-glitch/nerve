  <action>
    Create the Stripe account and begin business verification. Per D-09, start with personal identity — business details (Gewerbeschein, Geschäftskonto IBAN) are added as they become available. Stripe does not require full business verification to create an account; payouts are gated on verification, but account creation and configuration can begin now.

    **Steps:**
    1. Go to: https://dashboard.stripe.com/register
    2. Register with:
       - Email: use your primary business email (this will be the account owner email)
       - Country: Germany
       - Fill in personal details as prompted
    3. After creating the account, go to: Settings → Business settings (or "Activate your account")
    4. Fill in business information:
       - Business type: Individual (Einzelunternehmer) — NOT "Company"
       - Legal name: André Preuß
       - Address: your Iserlohn address
       - Business website: leave blank or put a placeholder (your production URL will be added in Phase 3)
       - Product description: "SaaS subscription for B2B sales training and live coaching"
    5. Identity verification:
       - Stripe will request a government-issued ID (Personalausweis) — upload a clear photo
       - If Stripe requests additional business documents (Gewerbeschein), upload the confirmation from Plan 01
    6. Bank account for payouts:
       - Do NOT add the bank account yet if Kontist IBAN is not yet available
       - If Kontist IBAN is already available (from Task 1): go to Settings → Bank accounts → Add bank account, enter the Kontist IBAN
       - Per D-10: Verify that the contracting entity shown is "Stripe Payments Europe, Ltd." — this is the EU entity and should be selected automatically for German registrations
    7. Enable Stripe Tax (optional at this stage):
       - This is NOT required now — skip for Phase 4
    8. Save the Stripe account ID (format: acct_XXXXXXXX) — visible in the Stripe Dashboard URL or Settings

    **What the account state will be after this task:**
    - Account created: YES
    - Identity verified: YES (or pending Stripe review, typically same day)
    - Payouts enabled: PENDING (requires Gewerbeschein + Geschäftskonto IBAN — add these as they become available)
    - This is the expected state. Payouts being "pending" does NOT block Phase 2 or Phase 3 work.

    **Note for Phase 4:**
    The Stripe account will need full business verification before going live. Phase 4 planning should assume the Stripe account may still be in "payouts pending" state and include a task to complete verification with the Gewerbeschein + Kontist IBAN.
