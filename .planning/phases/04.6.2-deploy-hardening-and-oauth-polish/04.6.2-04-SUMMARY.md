## Root cause (confirmed)

Hypothesis E from `04.6.2-04-ROOTCAUSE.md`: the test email from 04.6.1 was already registered with `onboarding_done=True`. User was silently redirected to `/profiles/wizard` via `/dashboard`, saw a different page and interpreted it as "onboarding buttons broken".

**No actual bug in onboarding code.** The fix adds observability so future regressions are diagnosable and defends against stale caches.
